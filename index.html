Frequently Asked Questions

What services can I monitor?

Any HTTP or HTTPS endpoint - websites, APIs, webhooks, health checks, or custom services.

Does it work with authentication?

Yes! Add custom headers including authorization tokens, API keys, or any headers your service requires.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All monitoring happens locally on your Mac. No data is sent to external servers.

What macOS versions are supported?

Lumenator requires macOS 12 (Monterey) or later.
